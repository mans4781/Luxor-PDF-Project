# About Luxor PDF

## Smarter PDF Tools. Secure Documents. Simpler Workflows.

Luxor PDF is a modern document productivity platform created to make working with PDF files simpler, faster, and more secure.

From opening and reviewing everyday documents to protecting confidential files and completing legally important signature workflows, Luxor PDF brings essential document tools together in one professional software ecosystem.

Our product suite includes:

### Luxor PDF Reader

A fast, reliable, and user-friendly PDF reader designed for comfortable everyday document viewing, navigation, annotation, printing, and management.

### Luxor PDF Secure

A powerful PDF security and document-management solution that helps users protect, organize, convert, compress, merge, split, extract, watermark, and securely share PDF files.

### Luxor PDF eSign

A streamlined electronic-signature solution designed to help individuals and businesses prepare, send, sign, track, and manage documents without relying on slow, paper-based processes.

# The Problems We Solve

## Complicated and Expensive PDF Software

Many PDF applications are either too limited for professional work or too complicated and expensive for everyday users.

Luxor PDF provides essential and advanced document capabilities through a cleaner, more approachable experience.

## Too Many Separate Applications

Users often need one application to read PDFs, another to edit or convert them, another to secure files, and another to collect signatures.

Luxor PDF brings these important workflows together through three connected products: Luxor PDF Reader, Luxor PDF Secure, and Luxor PDF eSign.

## Difficulty Managing PDF Pages

Rearranging, deleting, extracting, inserting, merging, or splitting PDF pages can be unnecessarily difficult.

Luxor PDF makes page-level document management faster and more intuitive.

## Large and Difficult-to-Share Files

Large PDF files can be difficult to upload, email, store, or share with clients and team members.

Our compression and document-optimization tools help reduce file size while maintaining practical document quality.

## Confidential Documents Shared Without Protection

Sensitive documents are frequently shared without adequate access controls.

Luxor PDF Secure helps users add passwords, restrict printing and copying, apply watermarks, set access conditions, and manage document availability.

## Slow Paper-Based Signature Processes

Printing, signing, scanning, and emailing documents creates delays, increases administrative work, and makes tracking difficult.

Luxor PDF eSign helps streamline the signing process so documents can move from preparation to completion more efficiently.

## Poor Visibility Into Document Status

Users often do not know whether a document has been opened, signed, completed, or is still waiting for action.

Luxor PDF eSign is designed to provide a clearer and more organized signing workflow.

## Inconsistent User Experience

Traditional PDF applications may contain crowded menus, outdated interfaces, and features that are difficult to locate.

Luxor PDF focuses on a modern, organized, and user-friendly interface that helps users find the right tool quickly.
