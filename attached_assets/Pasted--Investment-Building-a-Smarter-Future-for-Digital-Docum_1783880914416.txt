# Investment

## Building a Smarter Future for Digital Documents

Luxor PDF is building a modern document platform designed to make PDF reading, editing, protection, management and digital signing simpler for individuals, professionals and businesses.

Our goal is to create an accessible, secure and user-friendly alternative to complicated and expensive document software.

Luxor PDF is a brand owned and operated by **Fairnova Labs Private Limited**.

---

## Built Independently. Growing with Purpose.

Luxor PDF is currently a **fully bootstrapped business**.

Since the beginning, our product development, technology, infrastructure and brand-building activities have been funded internally. We have not received any external investment to date.

Building independently has enabled us to:

* Remain focused on genuine user needs
* Make long-term product decisions
* Develop without unnecessary outside pressure
* Maintain a clear and consistent product vision
* Build a strong foundation before pursuing rapid expansion

We are proud of the progress we have made through commitment, careful execution and internally funded development.

## The Luxor PDF Ecosystem

We are developing Luxor PDF as an integrated suite of document productivity and security solutions.

### Luxor PDF Reader

A modern desktop PDF reader designed to provide a fast, intuitive and professional document-viewing experience.

Luxor PDF Reader helps users open, read, navigate, annotate and manage PDF documents through a simple and user-friendly interface.

### Luxor PDF Secure

A document security solution designed to help users protect, control and securely manage sensitive PDF files.

Its capabilities are focused on document protection, access control, secure sharing and advanced PDF management.

### Luxor PDF eSign

**Luxor PDF eSign is currently in the development phase.**

It is being designed to simplify digital document signing for individuals, professionals and businesses.

Our objective is to create a secure and accessible eSignature experience that can work seamlessly with the wider Luxor PDF ecosystem.

Features, availability and launch timelines for Luxor PDF eSign will be announced as development progresses.

## The Opportunity

PDF documents remain essential across almost every industry, including:

* Business and professional services
* Education and research
* Legal and financial services
* Government and administration
* Healthcare
* Construction and engineering
* Remote work and digital collaboration

However, many existing PDF solutions are expensive, fragmented, overly complicated or dependent on multiple separate applications.

Luxor PDF aims to bring essential document capabilities into one connected and easy-to-use ecosystem.
